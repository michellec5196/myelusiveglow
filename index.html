ABOUT

CEO GLOW

Meet the face behind Elusive Glow Esthetics, Sofia Almazan. She started her Esthetician journey in fall of 2018, and had since been leaving some glow wherever she goes. Beyond the fundamental education of Esthetics, Sofia is dressed in many certifications and is constantly continuing to stay up to date with the growing beauty industry. She is always eager to learn more and do more, so she can grow and thrive as a skilled professional for her clients.

MORE THAN SKIN DEEP

Aside from skin and education, you can often find Sofia behind the table crafting eyelash extensions or prepping a client for their next wax. Sofia prides herself in: QUALITY, LUXURY, KNOWLEDGE, and most importantly PASSION.

BEYOND ESTHETICS

Sofia is a fulltime mom, and spends her days away from clients with her family. Whether she’s at the zoo or enjoying the views at the harbor- she is always with family. Sofia is also a huge advocate for self care, and loves to indulge in massages, pedicures, manicures, and all the feel good activities that we all deserve!
