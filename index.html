About Me

Hi I'm Sofia Danielle L.E. I am the owner & founder of Elusive Glow and its sister business, The Fairy Lash Tech.

I was born and raised in San Diego, CA an have been passionate about skincare since my teenage years. As soon as I was able to I went to school to pursue a career as an esthetician. I have been licensed for two years, last year I began doing lash extensions and found another passion in it. I want to help you not only have beautiful skin on the outside, but make sure its healthy inside too.
